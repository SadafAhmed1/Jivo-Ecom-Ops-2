Job Title: Junior Graphic Designer (Fresher)

Location: Remote / On-site (as per company policy)

Employment Type: Full-time

About Us:
We are a creative fashion and lifestyle brand specializing in trendy streetwear apparel under our flagship brand Grinkrawear. Our mission is to create bold, unique, and high-quality designs that resonate with modern youth culture. We are looking for a passionate and creative Junior Graphic Designer to join our growing team.

Job Summary:
As a Junior Graphic Designer, you will be responsible for creating visually compelling designs for apparel, social media, marketing materials, and other brand-related projects. You will work closely with the design team to develop ideas, create prototypes, and deliver final approved designs using tools like Canva, Adobe Illustrator, and Kittle.

Key Responsibilities:

Create and edit high-quality graphic designs for t-shirts, hoodies, and other apparel products.

Work on social media creatives, promotional banners, and marketing graphics.

Use design software such as Canva, Adobe Illustrator, and Kittle to produce designs according to brand guidelines.

Understand project briefs and deliver designs that meet the creative vision.

Collaborate with the creative team for brainstorming and concept development.

Revise designs based on feedback until final approval.

Prepare final design files for production.

Maintain an organized library of design assets and templates.

Assist in developing seasonal collections and campaign themes.

Skills & Qualifications:

Proficiency in Canva, Adobe Illustrator, and Kittle.

Basic understanding of typography, color theory, and layout design.

Strong attention to detail.

Ability to take constructive feedback and make revisions.

Creativity and willingness to experiment with fresh ideas.

Good communication and teamwork skills.

Ability to manage time and meet deadlines.

A portfolio (even academic or personal projects) is a plus.

Experience Required:

No professional experience required; freshers are welcome.

Design-related coursework or self-taught skills will be considered.

Growth Opportunities:
Upon successful completion of the probationary period and approval of initial designs, you will be assigned a new role with additional responsibilities, such as:

Leading small design projects.

Coordinating with marketing teams for campaign visuals.

Contributing to brand identity development.

Mentoring future interns or junior designers.

Perks & Benefits:

Opportunity to work on exciting fashion and lifestyle projects.

Skill development and on-the-job training.

Flexible working arrangements.

Creative freedom within brand guidelines.

Performance-based incentives.

Interested candidates should send their CV, a brief introduction, and a link to their design portfolio (if available) to [Your Company Email] with the subject line "Application for Junior Graphic Designer".

Note: We value creativity, originality, and a willingness to learn over prior work experience. If you have passion for design and fashion, we want to hear from you!
